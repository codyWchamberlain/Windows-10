Impression

When you login to your workstation, a logon script will add the first LAN Printer it finds in your office to your available printers. However, this will not occur when you are connected with VPN or using a local printer. There are also potential issues when traveling between offices that may prevent this from happening. To assist you with this, the following procedure can be used to manually add any LAN Printer to your workstation.

  • On the top right corner of your desktop, located in a small greenish box, click MyNetworkInfo
  • Select the Diagnostics tab
  • Under the Tools section on the right, click Browse \\F3xxxS01 (3xxx is your office id number)
  • Right click the desired printer and select Connect...
  • After a few seconds, the printer will download and install the appropriate drivers to your workstation. NOTE: The printer will not tell you if it was successfully installed.
    1. To confirm installation was successfull, click Start and select Devices and Printers
    2. You printer should be present on this page and may or may not be your new default printer
    3. To assign it as your default printer, right click on the printer and select Set as default printer

Contact Technical Support for any further issues installing your LAN Printer
