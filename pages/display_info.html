Display

Computer Only

The Computer Only Mode will do just as it says, display only on the workstation

Whether you have an external monitor plugged in or not, the display will remain on the workstation

Duplicate

The Duplicate Mode will copy your desktop windows to each screen.

Everything you have on one screen, will reflect to the other

Extend

The Extend Mode will extend your desktop windows to each side of the screen.

The Projector Only Mode will display on the extneral Monitor only. Your workstation monitor will remain blank whether it is turned on or not
